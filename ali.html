PILAU RECIPE

How pilau is being prepared

Ingredients

  • One big onion
  • Two tomatoes
  • One sachet of pilau masala
  • Salt
  • Garlic, coriander and ginger
  • 1/4 boiled meat
  • 1kg Rice
  • 6 cups of water

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in medium heat.put chopped onion and add garlic and ginger keep turnig until they turn brown
  2. Put the choped tomatoes, coriender and keep mixing. Add salt,masala to the mix
  3. keep adding water to avoid stickness and to form a paste
  4. Add the rice and mix for few seconds before pouring water
